About Me
I am Jules Leo Reserva. I live in the Philippines and I aspire to be a software engineer. At a very young age, I was already interested with computers. I used to play video games a lot back then, most notably a game called Minecraft.
In the game Minecraft, I've always loved being the guy in the friend group who've always found a way to efficiently managed redstone automation systems and tweaked on command blocks a little more often. Thus, over time it has developed into a hobby and skill. And soon thereafter, I've realized that what I've been doing inside of Minecraft had the same concept in Computer Science & Programming.
It was back in the year 2020, during the coronavirus outbreak, that I've found the Computer Science course called CS50x. At first, I was hesitant in taking on the course since I was still in high school and my schedule was already hectic. Later on, I soon got the courage and the curiosity to take the course head first since I didn't really know much about programming.
Now, I am currently a 2nd-year college student at Caraga Student University studying computer science. and have I have developed the discipline to always discover new ways to learn about programming, and hone my skills in self-studying computer science related courses, videos, new programming languages, etc.
